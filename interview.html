In conversation with Inga Kot

Where are you from? Do you miss it?

I’m from Moscow, Russia and sometimes I do miss it but not so much.

What are you studying at the academy?

I am studying Graphic Design.

What kind of job would you look for after you graduate and where?

I would like to be a graphic designer for the first 2-3 years, try different things, since graphic design is such a broad career, I like motion graphics and UX/UI design as well, so I have to decide what I actually want to do and which one I’d like more.

What do you like to do in your free time?

I do not get any free time since I am a student-athlete, it’s constant practices, treatment for injuries, classes and homework. I might go hang out with my friends on the weekend sometimes but it’s getting harder and harder to do because the basketball season has started.

Name your top 5 tv shows.

Dexter, Criminal Minds, Grey’s anatomy, How To Get Away With Murder, The Resident

If you could travel anywhere, where would you like to go?

I would love to go to Madagascar, Bahamas, England and Italy.

What's your favorite food?

My favorite food is curly fries, sometimes spicy, at the moment but it changes all the time.
